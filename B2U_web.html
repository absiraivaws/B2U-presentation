Process Flow

How B2U.App works with your bank

Secure, API-driven and fully controlled by the bank. B2U.App acts as a digital growth partner on top of your existing merchant accounts.

1
Bank issues LankaQR

Banks continue to onboard merchants and issue LankaQR codes as usual, with all KYC and compliance handled by the bank.

2
B2U.App merchant enablement

The bank shares the registered merchant list and grants a secure API to B2U.App for transaction verification.

3
Dynamic QR creation

Merchants log in to B2U.App and can generate dynamic QR for each bill or trip, including amount and reference.

4
Customer payment

Customers scan the QR using their preferred banking app or wallet and pay directly to the merchant’s bank account.

5
API verification

B2U.App checks the transaction via the bank’s API and confirms if the payment is successful and credited.

6
Instant merchant notification

Merchant receives real-time confirmation within B2U.App (and optional SMS / in-app alerts), reducing disputes and delays.

Stakeholder Value

Benefits for every participant in the ecosystem

One solution that creates value for merchants, customers, banks, CBSL, government and even the environment.

For Merchants

Micro, small and medium merchants, transport & services.

  • No need for petty cash or coins – exact amount collected via QR.
  • Avoid losses on small transactions (Rs. 300 per day and above accumulated).
  • Real-time credit to their existing bank account.
  • Verified transactions improve trust between merchant and customer.
  • Full transaction history at the bank – easier to prove turnover and apply for loans.
  • Lower cost compared to 3% card commission; less device cost and risk.
  • Any foreigner with a compatible app can pay easily via QR.
  • Zero risk of counterfeit notes or cash handling issues.

For Customers

Everyday citizens travelling, shopping, and paying bills.

  • No need to carry cash or cards – just use their mobile phone.
  • Seamless, fast QR payments with immediate confirmation.
  • Transactions authenticated through their trusted bank / wallet.
  • Clear digital records of even small value payments.
Banks

New business & lower cost

  • We bring incremental QR transactions and new merchant activity to the bank.
  • Reduce cash handling risk, counting cost and physical transport cost.
  • Decrease branch/operations workload; more digital self-service.
  • Better visibility of customer & merchant transaction patterns.
  • Each transaction can generate fee income (e.g. 1% or agreed model).
  • Strengthen low-cost deposits (CASA) through digital inflows.
Central Bank (CBSL)

Digital Sri Lanka acceleration

  • Less need to print, transport and destroy physical cash.
  • Supports CBSL’s digital payment and LankaQR roadmap.
  • Higher traceability and formalisation of the economy.
Government

Efficient & localised payments

  • Reduced public-sector cash handling and related costs.
  • Better understanding of income patterns for taxation and policy.
  • No need to send commission fees overseas (Visa/Master) – more value stays in Sri Lanka.
Environment

Green & sustainable
